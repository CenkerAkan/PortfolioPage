Register++

Used Skills: Java.

Register++ is a third party desktop app developed for education institutions. In education institutions, sections change process, when a place opens, the first person to see it has that place. This means section change process depends on timing. Register++ automates the section change process, students can create single or multi section change requests and when there is an empty place in desired section/s the request, system executes the first request that is made towards that empty space. In this project REST API, Mongodb, Java and Apache Netbeans is used. With this project, students do not have to wait in front of their computers for hours, refreshing the page for every 30 seconds, waiting for a place to open in the course section they desire. Register++ is developed within a semester with a team of five.

Heritage of Roots

Used Skills: Unity, C#.

Heritage of Roots is a two-dimensional endless runner game. In the game player controls a root of a tree and acoids colliding trash. The game emphasizes the pollution problem of todays world and how the humanity is differentiated from its roots. And the game offers a solution: remembering the heritage of roots. Beside of its message, Heritage of Roots contains power ups, different types of obstacles and moving obstacles. The majority of the sounds of the game is created by developer team. The game is developed by a team of six, working nonstop for two days, in the Game Circle Global Game Jam event.

Wind Tunnel Simulation

Used Skills: Unreal Engine 5.

Wind Tunnel Simulation is a project made with using Unreal Engine 5. This project is made for Infinia Software Development Hackathon, is made in 24 hours with non-stop working with a team of two. Wind tunnels are large tubes with air moving inside. The tunnels are used to copy the actions of an object in flight. In this project, user can move inside the wind tunnel, grab objects and expose them to the moving air and can observe their aerodynamic behavior.There are ten different different objects; including a plane-wing, a sports car and a sphere. User can change the speed of gas, heat and the gas density.
