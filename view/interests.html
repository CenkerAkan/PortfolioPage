Interests

I like wood carving and mosaic making. Both of these processes resemble software development. While creating a masterpiece, unpredictable errors may happen. These errors may change the momentary operations; however, the final goal always stays the same. I like wood carving and mosaic making because dealing with these errors forces me to think broader and differently. I enjoy dealing with these errors. Also, after finishing the masterpiece, I look at my masterpiece and think about the challenges I encountered during its making. Thinking about the final form of my masterpiece and how I overcome these challenges gives me peace, happiness, and satisfaction.
